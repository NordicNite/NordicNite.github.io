Scott Kirkland

Scott Kirkland is one of the founders of The Crystal Method, partnering with Ken Jordan. He moved to Los Angeles, California, following Ken to form the duo.

In 2017, Scott as The Crystal Method went on tour with Tool, and it was around this time, that he collaborated with Tool bass player, Justin Chancellor, on a TCM side project called Bandwidth.
